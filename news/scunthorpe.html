Scunthorpe to Halesowen Relocation

D&D International engineering have completed a major relocation project from Scunthorpe to Halesowen of a F&K 8 colour Flexographic printing machine. The project was completed on time, within the estimated budget and to the satisfaction of the customer.

The next major project to break out is a relocation of a Cobden Chadwick 6 colour Linerflex Flexographic printing machine from Australia to the USA. And also, as yet to be confirmed, another Cobdan Chadwick Linerflex Flexographic Printing Machine from Hungary to Tunisia.

A full order book until December to comply with our International name and the reputation of our expertise and experience.
