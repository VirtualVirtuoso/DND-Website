A truly international year

D&D Int. Eng. have had a truly international year work wise with relocation projects from/to the UK, Hungary, Germany, Greece, Algeria, Spain, Lebanon & Egypt. All of the projects were undertaken on a blue chip project management basis within budget and to the satisfaction of our customers.

Perhaps the most traumatic project was our visit to Cairo, Egypt, during which the riots kicked off and a curfew was imposed on the city from 19.00 to 6.00. We decided to leave the following morning to catch a 6.00 flight from Cairo and on our way to the airport, was held up by the rebels at various road blocks.

Fortunately they did not steal anything but it was a “squeaky bum” time experience; such are the travails of an international engineering company who are prepared to go the “extra mile” to undertake projects and succeed at our chosen profession.
